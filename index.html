For those that feel less familiar

If you’re new here or if it’s been a while, take a moment to notice where you are and how to find your way back. I don’t know whether you’ll bookmark this page or subscribe to my RSS feed to do so, but both of these things make coming back here as often as you like a simple process.

What can you read about here?

Though I tend to write about OpenBSD and Information Technology (IT), I find plenty of other subjects fascinating as well. Among them are music and psychology. But because I’m captivated by so many things, you’ll be reading for a while if I list them all out.

All the same, feel free to continue scrolling down. Sometimes discovering what you have in common with someone else can pique your interest and curiosity.
